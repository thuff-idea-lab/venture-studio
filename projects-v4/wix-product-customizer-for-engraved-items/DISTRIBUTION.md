## 3. Validation Criteria

- **Continue building if:** 50 sign-ups for the free trial within the first week.
- **Pivot if:** Users express interest but provide feedback indicating the product doesn't solve their problem (e.g., "I need more features" or "This isn't what I was looking for").
- **Kill if:** Fewer than 10 sign-ups for the free trial within the first week or consistent negative feedback indicating the product is not useful.

## 4. Week 2-4 Playbook

### If traction is working:
- **What to double down on:** Continue engaging in the communities where you received the most traction. Create more posts sharing user testimonials and success stories.
- **What content to create:** Develop a tutorial video demonstrating how to use the widget effectively and share it on social media and the landing page.
- **How to convert early users to paying customers:** After the trial period, send a follow-up email offering a discount for the first month of support packages.
- **When and how to ask for feedback:** Send a feedback survey via email after the trial ends, asking users what they liked and what could be improved.

### If traction is NOT working:
- **How to diagnose what's wrong:** Review user feedback to see if the product lacks essential features or if the messaging is unclear.
- **What to try differently:** Consider adjusting the pricing model or offering a more extended free trial period to entice users.
- **When to officially kill it:** If there are fewer than 5 active users after two weeks of the launch and no clear path to improvement based on feedback.
