## 3. Validation Criteria

- **Continue building if:** 50+ unique visitors to the landing page and 5+ purchases within the first week.
- **Pivot if:** There are 20+ inquiries but no purchases, indicating interest but potential issues with pricing or value proposition.
- **Kill if:** Less than 10 unique visitors and no purchases or inquiries within the first week.

## 4. Week 2-4 Playbook

### If traction is working:
- **What to double down on:** Continue posting in the same communities and share user testimonials.
- **What content to create:** Create a short video tutorial on how to integrate the template into Webflow.
- **How to convert early users to paying customers:** Offer a limited-time discount to early adopters for referrals.
- **When and how to ask for feedback:** After the first week of sales, send an email to buyers asking for feedback on the product and their experience.

### If traction is NOT working:
- **How to diagnose what's wrong:** Analyze traffic sources and engagement metrics. Check if the landing page has a high bounce rate.
- **What to try differently:** Adjust the messaging on the landing page or offer a limited-time discount to create urgency.
- **When to officially kill it:** If no traction is gained after 4 weeks, consider halting the project and reassessing the product-market fit.
