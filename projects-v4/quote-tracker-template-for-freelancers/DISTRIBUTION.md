## 3. Validation Criteria
- **Continue building if:** 50+ purchases of the template within the first 2 weeks.
- **Pivot if:** Engagement is high (100+ comments across all channels) but purchases are low (less than 10). Consider adjusting the pricing or marketing angle.
- **Kill if:** Less than 5 purchases within the first 2 weeks and low engagement (less than 20 comments total).

## 4. Week 2-4 Playbook

### If traction is working:
- **What to double down on:** Continue posting in the same communities and consider creating a short video tutorial on how to use the template.
- **What content to create:** Blog post on "How to Efficiently Track Quotes as a Freelancer" that links back to the template.
- **How to convert early users to paying customers:** Send a follow-up email to users who downloaded the free sample, offering a discount on the full template.
- **When and how to ask for feedback:** After 2 weeks, send an email to buyers asking for feedback on the template and suggestions for improvements.

### If traction is NOT working:
- **How to diagnose what's wrong:** Review engagement metrics and customer feedback to identify if the product, messaging, or audience targeting is off.
- **What to try differently:** Experiment with different post angles or formats (e.g., infographics or videos) to see what resonates more.
- **When to officially kill it:** If no purchases are made after 4 weeks despite multiple marketing efforts and engagement, consider discontinuing the product.
