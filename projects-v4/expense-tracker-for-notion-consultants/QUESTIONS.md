# Pre-Build Questions for Expense Tracker for Notion Consultants

Answer these questions before handing the spec to your coding agent. For each question, a suggested default is provided — accept it or override it.

## Branding & Identity
### What will be the name of the product?
**Why it matters:** The name is crucial for branding and marketing efforts, as it will be how users identify and refer to the product.  
**Suggested default:** "Notion Expense Tracker"

### Do you want a custom domain for the product?
**Why it matters:** A custom domain can enhance credibility and make it easier for users to find and remember the product.  
**Suggested default:** Yes, use "notionexpensetracker.com"

### Do you have a logo or branding guidelines?
**Why it matters:** Consistent branding helps in establishing a professional image and can impact user trust and engagement.  
**Suggested default:** No, proceed with a simple text-based logo for now.

### What tagline do you want to use?
**Why it matters:** A tagline can succinctly communicate the product's value proposition and attract potential users.  
**Suggested default:** "Simplify Your Business Finances"

## Product Behavior
### What categories should be included for expense tracking?
**Why it matters:** Predefined categories help users organize their expenses effectively and improve the usability of the template.  
**Suggested default:** "Travel, Software, Marketing, Office Supplies, Meals"

### Should users be able to customize categories?
**Why it matters:** Allowing customization can enhance user satisfaction and adaptability of the template to various business needs.  
**Suggested default:** Yes, users can add or modify categories.

### What type of visualizations do you want to include in the reports?
**Why it matters:** The choice of visualizations can significantly affect how users interpret their financial data and make decisions.  
**Suggested default:** Pie charts for category breakdowns and bar graphs for monthly expenses.

## Monetization Setup
### What payment processor will you use?
**Why it matters:** The choice of payment processor affects how transactions are handled and can impact user experience.  
**Suggested default:** Stripe

### Will you offer a money-back guarantee?
**Why it matters:** A money-back guarantee can increase trust and reduce buyer hesitation, potentially boosting sales.  
**Suggested default:** Yes, a 14-day money-back guarantee.

## Content & Data
### What initial copy do you want for the product description?
**Why it matters:** Clear and compelling copy is essential for marketing and helps users understand the product's benefits.  
**Suggested default:** "Easily track and manage your business expenses with our Notion template designed specifically for consultants."

### Do you want to include sample data in the template?
**Why it matters:** Sample data can help users understand how to use the template effectively and make it easier for them to get started.  
**Suggested default:** Yes, include sample expenses for each category.

## Accounts & Services
### Do you need an email provider for customer communications?
**Why it matters:** An email provider is essential for managing customer support and marketing communications effectively.  
**Suggested default:** Yes, use Mailchimp.

### Will you set up analytics to track user engagement?
**Why it matters:** Analytics can provide insights into user behavior, helping to inform future updates and marketing strategies.  
**Suggested default:** Yes, use Google Analytics.
