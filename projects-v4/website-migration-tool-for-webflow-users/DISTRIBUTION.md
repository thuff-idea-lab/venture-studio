## 3. Validation Criteria

- **Continue building if:** You receive 50 sign-ups for the free trial within the first 7 days.
- **Pivot if:** Users express interest but provide feedback that the tool needs significant changes (e.g., "I love the idea, but it doesn't handle X well").
- **Kill if:** You receive fewer than 10 sign-ups and no engagement or feedback within the first week.

## 4. Week 2-4 Playbook

### If traction is working:
- **What to double down on:** Focus on engaging with users who signed up, offering personalized support, and encouraging them to share their experiences in their networks.
- **What content to create:** Write a blog post or create a video tutorial on how to use the tool effectively.
- **How to convert early users to paying customers:** After the trial, reach out via email to offer a discount for the first paid migration project.
- **When and how to ask for feedback:** After the trial period ends, send a follow-up email asking for feedback and suggestions for improvement.

### If traction is NOT working:
- **How to diagnose what's wrong:** Analyze user feedback to determine if the tool's functionality is lacking or if the messaging is off.
- **What to try differently:** Adjust the messaging to better highlight the time-saving benefits or target a different audience segment.
- **When to officially kill it:** If after 4 weeks you have fewer than 20 sign-ups and no clear path to improvement, consider discontinuing the project.
