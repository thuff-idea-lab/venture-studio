## 3. Validation Criteria

- **Continue building if:** You receive 50+ visits to the landing page and 10+ purchases within the first 7 days.
- **Pivot if:** You receive feedback that the product is useful, but users want additional features (e.g., automated calculations) or a different format (e.g., Google Sheets).
- **Kill if:** You receive less than 20 visits to the landing page and no purchases within the first week.

## 4. Week 2-4 Playbook

### If traction is working:
- **What to double down on:** Increase engagement in successful channels (e.g., post more frequently in r/freelance and the Facebook group).
- **What content to create:** Create tutorial videos showing how to use the spreadsheet effectively and share them on social media.
- **How to convert early users to paying customers:** Offer a limited-time discount for those who provide feedback and testimonials.
- **When and how to ask for feedback:** After the first week of sales, send an email to buyers asking for their thoughts and suggestions for improvement.

### If traction is NOT working:
- **How to diagnose what's wrong:** Analyze traffic sources and engagement metrics. If low, consider if the product is not resonating with the audience or if the messaging is off.
- **What to try differently:** Experiment with different post titles and angles, focusing on specific pain points freelancers face.
- **When to officially kill it:** If after 4 weeks, you have less than 100 visits to the landing page and fewer than 5 purchases, consider discontinuing the product.
