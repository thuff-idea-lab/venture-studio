## 3. Validation Criteria
- **Continue building if:** 50 sign-ups for the free trial within 7 days.
- **Pivot if:** Feedback indicates users like the concept but find the tool difficult to use or lacking features.
- **Kill if:** Less than 10 sign-ups within 7 days and no meaningful engagement from users.

## 4. Week 2-4 Playbook

### If traction is working:
- **What to double down on:** Increase engagement in communities where users are active, share user testimonials, and encourage word-of-mouth referrals.
- **What content to create:** Blog posts on cocktail menu tips, user success stories, and how-to guides for using the tool.
- **How to convert early users to paying customers:** Offer a limited-time discount for the first 20 users who convert from free trials to paid.
- **When and how to ask for feedback:** After one week of usage, send a survey to users asking for specific feedback on features and usability.

### If traction is NOT working:
- **How to diagnose what's wrong:** Analyze user feedback to determine if the product features are insufficient or if the messaging is unclear.
- **What to try differently:** Experiment with different messaging angles in posts and consider offering a more interactive demo.
- **When to officially kill it:** If after 4 weeks there are fewer than 20 sign-ups and minimal engagement, consider discontinuing the project.
