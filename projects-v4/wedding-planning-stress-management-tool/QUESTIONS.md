# Pre-Build Questions for Wedding Planning Stress Management Tool

Answer these questions before handing the spec to your coding agent. For each question, a suggested default is provided — accept it or override it.

## Branding & Identity

### What will be the name of the product?
**Why it matters:** The name is crucial for branding and marketing; it should resonate with the target audience.  
**Suggested default:** "Cultural Wedding Planner"

### What domain name will you use for the product?
**Why it matters:** A memorable domain name is essential for online presence and marketing.  
**Suggested default:** culturalweddingplanner.com

### Do you have a logo or branding guidelines in mind?
**Why it matters:** Consistent branding helps establish trust and recognition among users.  
**Suggested default:** Use a simple logo featuring a wedding ring and multicultural symbols.

### What tagline do you want to use?
**Why it matters:** A tagline succinctly communicates the product's value proposition and can enhance marketing efforts.  
**Suggested default:** "Simplifying Your Multicultural Wedding Journey"

## Product Behavior

### What core features must be included in the checklist?
**Why it matters:** Defining core features ensures the product meets user needs and expectations.  
**Suggested default:** Include tasks for venue selection, catering, attire, and cultural rituals.

### How should the timeline feature be structured?
**Why it matters:** The timeline is critical for helping users manage their planning effectively.  
**Suggested default:** A linear timeline with milestones set at 6 months, 3 months, 1 month, and 1 week before the wedding.

### What budgeting categories should be included?
**Why it matters:** Clear budgeting categories help users allocate funds effectively and avoid overspending.  
**Suggested default:** Venue, catering, attire, decorations, and entertainment.

## Monetization Setup

### How will you set up payment processing?
**Why it matters:** A smooth payment process is essential for user satisfaction and revenue generation.  
**Suggested default:** Use Stripe for payment processing.

### Will you offer a free trial or demo version?
**Why it matters:** A free trial can attract users and encourage them to convert to paying customers.  
**Suggested default:** No free trial; offer a money-back guarantee within 30 days.

## Content & Data

### What initial content will be included in the app?
**Why it matters:** Initial content sets the tone for user experience and can help in user retention.  
**Suggested default:** A sample wedding checklist and budget template for a multicultural wedding.

### Do you have specific copy for the landing page?
**Why it matters:** Effective copy can significantly impact conversion rates and user engagement.  
**Suggested default:** Use clear, concise language that highlights the tool's benefits and features.

## Accounts & Services

### Which analytics service will you use to track user behavior?
**Why it matters:** Understanding user behavior is crucial for improving the product and marketing strategies.  
**Suggested default:** Google Analytics.

### What email service provider will you use for user communication?
**Why it matters:** A reliable email service is essential for onboarding, updates, and customer support.  
**Suggested default:** Mailchimp.
