## 3. Validation Criteria
- **Continue building if:** 100 sign-ups for early access within the first 7 days.
- **Pivot if:** Users express interest but cite specific features they need that are missing (e.g., mobile app, more integrations).
- **Kill if:** Less than 20 sign-ups within the first 7 days, indicating low interest in the product.

## 4. Week 2-4 Playbook

### If traction is working:
- **What to double down on:** Continue engaging with users who signed up, ask for feedback, and refine the product based on their input.
- **What content to create:** Create tutorial videos or blog posts on how to maximize productivity with the tool.
- **How to convert early users to paying customers:** Offer a limited-time discount for the first month of subscription to early users.
- **When and how to ask for feedback:** After users have had a week to try the product, send a follow-up email asking for specific feedback on features and usability.

### If traction is NOT working:
- **How to diagnose what's wrong:** Analyze user feedback to determine if the product features meet their needs or if the messaging is unclear.
- **What to try differently:** Adjust the messaging to better highlight pain points or consider adding features that users are requesting.
- **When to officially kill it:** If after 4 weeks you have less than 50 sign-ups and no clear path to improvement based on user feedback, consider discontinuing the project.
